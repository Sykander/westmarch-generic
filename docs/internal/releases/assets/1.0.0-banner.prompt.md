# 1.0.0 Release Banner Prompt

Use this prompt to generate a wide release banner. Keep the generated image free of text and logos; overlay the committed `westmarch-generic` logo separately so it stays crisp and accurate.

```text
Create an original wide banner image for the westmarch-generic 1.0.0 release.

Format: 1600x500 landscape banner, high-resolution digital painting, suitable for a GitHub release header.

Visual direction: classic high-fantasy tabletop RPG location illustration, similar in mood and finish to polished Forgotten Realms location artwork, but fully original. Avoid copying any official map, city, monster, character, logo, or symbol.

Scene: a lively frontier westmarch crossroads beside a river and forest edge. The location should feel like a shared adventuring hub where multiple D&D adventurers are doing the activities supported by the 1.0.0 release.

Include these activity vignettes in one cohesive scene:
- explorers leaving a gate with a map and compass toward woodland ruins;
- one adventurer fishing from the riverbank;
- a small crafting table or forge where someone is brewing, scribing, enchanting, or repairing gear;
- a market stall where adventurers are buying and selling supplies;
- a wagon, horse, boat, or signposted road suggesting travel between locations;
- a noticeboard or quest board with unreadable parchment shapes;
- subtle hints of time, weather, and wilderness encounters through clouds, sunlight, tracks, and distant silhouettes.

Composition: cinematic wide establishing shot, readable at small size, not too crowded. Leave clean negative space in the upper-left or upper-center for a small project logo overlay after generation. Put the busiest detail toward the lower third and side edges.

Lighting and mood: bright heroic daylight with warm lantern accents, inviting and adventurous, clear contrast, natural greens, river blues, weathered wood, brass, parchment, and stone. Avoid a one-color palette.

Style: painterly but clean, book-location art, detailed environment, realistic fantasy proportions, grounded clothing and gear, no modern objects.

Text and logos: no readable text, no title, no watermark, no D&D ampersand, no official Forgotten Realms or Wizards of the Coast branding.
```
