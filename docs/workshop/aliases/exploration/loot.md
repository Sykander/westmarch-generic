# loot

`!loot <creature>` starts a loot session. `!loot <item>` rolls to recover one lootable entry. `!loot` shows the active session, and `!loot clear` ends it.

Config:

- `subsystems.exploration.config.monster_images.loot`: `thumbnail`, `image`, or `off`
- `subsystems.exploration.config.show_check_dcs.loot`: `True` or `False`
