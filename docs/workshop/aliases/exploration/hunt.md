# hunt

`!hunt <creature> [party_size] [bonuses]`

Tracks a creature with a Survival check. On success, the embed includes the combat init suggestion and may include monster art from the catalogue.

Config:

- `subsystems.exploration.config.monster_images.hunt`: `thumbnail`, `image`, or `off`
- `subsystems.exploration.config.show_check_dcs.hunt`: `True` or `False`
