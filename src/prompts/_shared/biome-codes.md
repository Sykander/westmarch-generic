# Allowed biome codes

Copy into prompts when updating the canonical list. Also embedded in location, path, and biome pool prompts.

`beach`, `forest`, `mountain`, `cave`, `ruins`, `road`, `urban`, `river`, `sea`, `plains`, `desert`, `swamp`, `sky`, `deep_seas`, `underdark`, `tundra`, `jungle`, `volcanic`, `astral`

Do **not** invent codes (`coast`, `woodland`, `city`, …).

Engine preset modules: `src/gvars/configs/biomes/<code>.gvar` — referenced as `engine:configs/biomes/<code>` in config.
